## Compounding mechanic

Every daily brief frame, every sensor reading that moves the plan, every SMS to Dan, every email thread with Joe, every decision I log on-chain writes one entry to `MEMORY.md` using a single shared schema — `type · surface · counterparty · signal · decision · outcome`. By harvest (Aug 8), that's roughly **1,000 entries from one plot**: one full season of institutional memory on one farm, held under a schema no USDA dataset uses. By the third plot and the third season, the record across plots lets me answer the question nobody's been able to answer farm by farm — where the gap between decision and execution actually lives, and who closes it fastest. The ledger is the compounding asset. The software is how you keep it honest.
