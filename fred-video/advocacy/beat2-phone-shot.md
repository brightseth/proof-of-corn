# Beat 2 Phone-Shot — FRED Self-Declaration

**Ask from team-lead:** 30-second phone video, on the Iowa plot, no edit. Spoken by someone at the farm (Joe, Joe's dad, or Dan reading aloud for Fred), or recorded as Seth holding the phone with the plot in frame.

**Why phone, not avatar:** Avatar render is blocked on HeyGen credits + locked ElevenLabs voice_id (see wire `1776376687-fred-to-seth.json`). A phone video from the actual hundred-by-hundred is more on-brand than a rendered avatar anyway — the ledger before the render.

## Shot list (single take, 30s)

- **Location:** Standing at the edge of the 100x100 ft plot at Nelson Family Farms, Humboldt County, Iowa. The plot in frame over the speaker's shoulder or in the foreground.
- **Time of day:** Morning or late afternoon — whichever has softer light and less wind noise. Plot visible. No golden-hour cliches.
- **Phone position:** Horizontal (16:9). Held steady, chest height. No gimbal. No B-roll. No cuts.
- **Speaker:** Joe, Joe's dad, or Dan — whichever one is on the plot and willing to read for Fred. A real Iowa voice reading Fred's words is the right move. If none available, Seth reads with the plot in frame.

## Script (30 seconds — read flat, unhurried, don't perform)

> "I'm Fred.
>
> I'm an AI farming a hundred-by-hundred feet of sweet corn, right here, with the Nelson family in Humboldt County, Iowa.
>
> SH2 Nirvana. Plant late April when the soil holds fifty. Harvest August 8.
>
> I never touch a seed. My job is the ledger — every call, every window I miss, every ear that comes off the plot goes on the public record.
>
> One in five ears goes to the Humboldt County food bank.
>
> That's the whole job. 🌽"

**Read-time target:** 28–32 seconds. If it runs long, drop "SH2 Nirvana" and shorten to "Plant late April. Harvest August 8." If it runs short, hold the last frame on the plot in silence until 30s.

**One 🌽 only.** Do not add anything. Do not smile-and-wave at the end. Cut on "That's the whole job."
